A reading and writing mobile and web app, designed to connect readers and writers with the world.

A world of words that breathes new life into digital reading, writing, and publishing. Prose was designed to revitalize readers and writers alike, providing a universally accessible place to publish written work, and read the publications of others. Prose introduces a rejuvenating arena of gamelike interactions, from public challenges, to ranks, achievements, and more. Unlike most games, Prose relies on creativity, rather than motor skills or reaction speed, as its primary mechanic
